% Information of the VR2Market_BaiaoTrial dataset %

Overview: Dataset that contains simultaneous GPS traces collected at 1 Hz from a team of firefighters during a forest fire exercise. The traces were generated by Android phones placed in each of four firefighters and a generic GPS device placed in the firetruck.

Traces/Files format: Latitude, Longitude, Timestamp

Traces/Files:

* dataFF1.txt: traces of Firefighter number 1.

* dataFF2.txt: traces of Firefighter number 2.

* dataFF3.txt: traces of Firefighter number 3.

* dataFF4.txt: traces of Firefighter number 4.

* dataTruck.txt: traces of firefighter truck.
